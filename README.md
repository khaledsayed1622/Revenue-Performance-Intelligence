# 📊 Revenue Performance Intelligence Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a **Power BI Sales Performance Dashboard** designed to analyze business performance using revenue, profit, and year-over-year comparisons.

The dashboard helps businesses monitor key metrics, identify growth trends, and evaluate performance across products, customers, cities, and sales channels.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into **actionable business insights** that support strategic decision-making.

---

# 📁 Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales data with multiple related tables.

Main tables include:

* Sales
* Customers
* Products
* Regions
* Date Table

A **Star Schema data model** was implemented to improve performance and analytical flexibility.

---

# 🧠 Data Modeling

The project uses a **Star Schema** model:

### Fact Table

* Sales

### Dimension Tables

* Customers
* Products
* Regions
* Date Table

This structure enables efficient filtering and time-based analysis such as **Year-over-Year comparisons**.

---

# ❓ Business Questions

The dashboard was designed to answer several key business questions:

1. What are the total sales and profit generated by the business?
2. What is the overall profit margin?
3. How does the current year's performance compare to the previous year?
4. Which products generate the highest sales?
5. Which cities generate the most revenue?
6. Which sales channels are the most profitable?
7. Who are the top-performing customers?
8. Are sales improving or declining compared to the previous year?

---

# 📊 Key Metrics (2019)

* **Total Sales:** 48.53M
* **Total Profit:** 18.32M
* **Profit Margin:** 37.76%
* **Products Sold:** 21K

---

# 🔍 Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Decline in Sales Compared to Previous Year

Sales in **2019 decreased by 10.17% compared to 2018**.

This decline suggests that the business experienced reduced demand, loss of customers, or changes in market conditions during 2019.

---

### 2️⃣ Profit Also Declined

Profit decreased by **8.01% compared to the previous year**.

Although both sales and profit declined, the drop in profit was **slightly smaller than the drop in sales**, which may indicate improvements in cost management or pricing strategy.

---

### 3️⃣ Profit Margin Remains Healthy

Despite the decline in revenue, the business maintained a **profit margin of 37.76%**, indicating strong profitability and efficient cost control.

---

### 4️⃣ Product Performance

A small number of products contribute significantly to overall sales, highlighting the importance of focusing on high-performing products.

---

### 5️⃣ Regional Performance

Cities such as **Christchurch, Waitakere, and Hamilton** generate the highest sales, indicating strong demand in these regions.

---

### 6️⃣ Sales Channels

The **Wholesale channel generates the highest profit**, while the **Export channel contributes the least**, suggesting an opportunity for improvement in international sales.

---

### 7️⃣ Key Customers

Customers such as **Medline, Capweld, and Pure Group** generate a significant portion of revenue and represent strategic business relationships.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### Investigate the Sales Decline in 2019

Analyze potential causes of the 10% decline in sales, such as reduced demand, increased competition, or changes in customer purchasing behavior.

---

### Strengthen Customer Retention

Focus on maintaining relationships with key customers through loyalty programs and strategic partnerships.

---

### Focus on High-Performing Products

Increase marketing efforts and inventory for the best-selling products to maximize revenue.

---

### Expand Export Channel

Since export contributes the least profit, exploring new international markets could create additional revenue opportunities.

---

### Leverage High-Demand Regions

Invest more marketing resources in top-performing cities to further increase sales.
